# Code of Conduct

This repository is a public portfolio hub and lightweight static site.

## Our standards
- Be respectful, professional, and constructive.
- Avoid harassment, hate speech, threats, or doxxing.
- Do not post private or sensitive information (yours or anyone elseâ€™s).
- Keep issues and pull requests relevant to improving this site.

## Scope
These rules apply in issues, pull requests, and public discussions on this repository.

## Reporting
If you believe someone is behaving inappropriately, open an issue with minimal detail or contact the maintainer directly via the email listed on the site (once provided). Do not include sensitive personal data.

## Enforcement
The maintainer may remove content or restrict participation to keep the project safe and constructive.
